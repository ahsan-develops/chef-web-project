Meet the Chef

Chef Alexandre Morel

Born in Lyon and trained under the great Pierre Gagnaire, Chef Alexandre Morel has spent over 15 years redefining what fine dining means. His philosophy is simple: respect the ingredient, trust the technique, and feed the soul.

Today, Maison Élara is the culmination of that journey — a space where French classicism meets global curiosity, and where every reservation feels like a personal invitation.

What We Offer

Our Services

From intimate dinners to grand celebrations, we craft every experience with equal devotion and precision.

Make an Enquiry
🕯
Dine-In Experience
Savour our full tasting menu in an intimate setting. Our dining room seats 42 guests, ensuring every visit is unhurried and personal.
📦
Takeaway & Delivery
Our signature dishes — thoughtfully packaged to retain every flavour note. Available Thursday through Sunday evenings.
🎉
Private Catering
Chef Alexandre and his team bring the Élara experience to your home, boardroom, or private event space for up to 200 guests.
💻
Online Ordering
Pre-order seasonal meal kits and curated hampers. Each kit includes detailed chef instructions and seasonal produce sourced that week.
👨‍🍳
Chef's Table
An exclusive counter experience for up to 6 guests, watching Chef Alexandre work in real time with a personalised tasting menu.
🍾
Celebrations & Events
Anniversaries, proposals, corporate dinners — our events team will tailor every detail from florals to custom menu printing.
